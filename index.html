Which date palm varieties do you supply?+

Our highlighted varieties are Aseel and Qarbala, with availability across different height bands. Contact us for current nursery stock.

Can you deliver outside Khairpur?+

Delivery feasibility depends on tree size, quantity and destination. Send your city and requirements for a transport assessment.

Do you provide professional installation?+

Yes. Supply and professional palm installation are core services. Site conditions are reviewed before final quotation.

How do I choose the right palm height?+

We consider your intended visual impact, access, transport, budget and site conditions before recommending a size.

How quickly will you respond?+

WhatsApp is the fastest route. Include your location, quantity and desired height so we can respond more accurately.

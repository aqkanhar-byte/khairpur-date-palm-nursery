What buyers need to know

01

Who is it for?

Aseel suits agricultural buyers, farmhouses and landscape projects seeking a date palm with authentic Khairpur provenance.

  • Orchard enquiries
  • Statement landscape planting

02

What affects the quotation?

Palm height alone is not enough. Quantity, access, destination and installation scope materially affect handling and transport.

  • Required quantity
  • Delivery city and access
  • Supply-only or installation

Before you order
